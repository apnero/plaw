Connecticut Vehicular Assault Attorney

While some motor vehicle offenses, such as DUIs, are charged as misdemeanors and a felony for subsequent convictions, vehicular assault is serious enough to be charged as a felony in Connecticut. A conviction on a charge of vehicular assault could result in a lengthy license suspension or revocation, large fines and incarceration. Experienced criminal defense is critical if you have been arrested for vehicular assault.
