Criminal Defense Attorney

There are two sides to every story. On one side, police officers and criminal prosecutors will use considerable resources to tell their version of events and secure a conviction. On the other side is a person who is usually unfamiliar with the criminal justice system and is worried about what impact an arrest will have on the future.

In order to level the playing field and ensure that your side of the story is heard, choose an experienced attorney to defend your rights. At Paoletti & Gusmano, Attorneys at Law, our Bridgeport lawyers have more than 50 years of combined trial experience defending individuals who have been arrested on a wide range of misdemeanor and felony crimes.

Even when it looks like the odds are stacked against you, there can still be a positive outcome to your case. Take the first step by calling our office in Bridgeport at 203-583-8585 to schedule a free, no-obligation consultation. We also serve the communities of New Haven, Waterbury, Stamford, Norwalk, Danbury, Stratford, Milford, Hartford, New Britain, West Hartford, Meriden and Bristol.

Experienced Criminal Defense in Bridgeport, CT

From a DWI charge to an arrest for a violent crime, any criminal conviction can have an impact on your livelihood. Our firm provides criminal defense for drug charges, sexual assault, Internet crimes and other offenses in Connecticut, including federal crimes. We will use creative techniques to uncover key evidence, analyze the facts of the case and develop an effective defense strategy.

The sooner you speak with a lawyer, the more that can be done to protect your rights. Our firm has achieved successful case results for decades on behalf of clients throughout Connecticut. While past results do not guarantee future ones, our experience and knowledge of criminal law allows us to tailor our representation to the situation and the needs of a client.
